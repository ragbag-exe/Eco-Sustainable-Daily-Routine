⏰ 6:30 AM – Wake Up Freshen up, hydrate, and do a short 10–20 min workout or stretch.

☕ 7:00 AM – Breakfast + Me Time Eat a solid breakfast. Optional: Read, journal, or catch up on news/podcasts.

👔 8:00 AM – Get Ready & Commute (if applicable) Dress, prep anything needed for the day, and head to work.

🖥️ 9:00 AM – Work Begins Focus on top priorities in the morning when your energy is high.

☕ 11:00 AM – Quick Break Take 5–10 minutes to walk, stretch, or grab coffee.

🍱 12:30 PM – Lunch Step away from your desk. Eat something balanced and refresh your mind.

🧠 1:00 PM – Back to Work Knock out tasks, attend meetings, and finish up your to-dos.

🕔 5:00 PM – Work Ends Shut down work, reflect on what got done, and log off.

🏃 5:30 PM – Exercise / Walk / Hobby Move your body, go to the gym, take a walk, or dive into a hobby.

🍽️ 7:00 PM – Dinner Relax with a good meal. Eat mindfully, maybe chat with family or friends.

🛋️ 8:00 PM – Unwind / Personal Time Watch a show, read, work on a side project, or just chill.

💤 10:30 PM – Get Ready for Bed Light wind-down routine: skin care, light reading, or breathing exercises.

😴 11:00 PM – Sleep Aim for 7–8 hours to stay energized the next day.
